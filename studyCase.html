About The Project

Cafe Travel is currently facing challenges in managing sales data which is still done manually, often causing recording errors, delays in decision making, and less efficiency in daily operations. Therefore, it is necessary to develop a sales system that can automate this process, which is expected to not only be able to record sales transactions but also assist in sales analysis, and create accurate and timely reports. In addition, as part of efforts to increase competitiveness and attract more customers, Cafe Travel also provides product customization features, especially on the pizza menu, which allows customers to choose and arrange their own ingredients and toppings according to their preferences. In this way, it is hoped that customer satisfaction can increase because they get products that suit their individual tastes and needs. In developing this sales system and product customization, user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) aspects play an important role. A system design that is creative, easy to use, and visually attractive will provide a pleasant experience for users. Focusing on UI/UX not only aims to facilitate operations but also to increase customer satisfaction through better interaction with the system.
